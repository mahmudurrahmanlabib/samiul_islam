    Project Name: Corporate Video

    Date of Project: Spring 2022

     

    Project Description:

    Based on the CI theme ‘Multiculturalism’ we are assigned to make a corporate video in a group. For this we made a group of three people and decided to work on Pathao food delivery service. Here we were asked to create a company, its logo and details of the company, client details and competitor analysis, background study, script writing and lastly an OVC for the international Mother’s Day.

    In this OVC we showed how a young school going boy fulfill his dream and break the multicultural stereotype that exist in the society.

     

    Project Justification:
    In Our country people are very conservative that they this only lower class or needy people can do the lower work like pathao food delivery. They don’t give value to the delivery or similar kind of job and by this kind of thought they create multicultural deviation. So, to prove it wrong we decided to work on this project and showed that anyone can do this job and every job deserve respect.

    Company Background (Pathao)

     

    Pathao Ltd was founded in 2015 the fastest tech startup in Asia.  Pathao Ltd provides an app-based through ride sharing, food delivery, parcel, e-commerce logistics services, courier and health. Pathao Ltd is the first major ride-sharing company in Bangladesh to get an enlistment certificate from the authorities. 

     

    Pathao Ltd started its first journey as a delivery service only in 2015. Later on, they started a bike-sharing service in mid-2016 and signed up more than 1,00,00 drivers and 1 million users in March 2018. Pathao currently has over 50,000 bikes registered on its platform and the company is valued over $100 million as of April 2018. Pathao was given Operation License in February 2018. On 3 December, Pathao became the first major ride-sharing service in Bangladesh to get an enlistment certificate from Bangladesh Road & Transport Authority (BRTA). In September 2018, Pathao launched its services in Nepal. Pathao is the first Bangladeshi company to offer On-demand Transport Sharing services abroad. On 3 December 2019, Pathao became the first major ride-sharing service providing company in Bangladesh to get an enlistment certificate from the authorities in Bangladesh. 

     

    Pathao was founded in 2015 by Hussain Elius, Shifat Adnan and Fahim Saleh. On November 1, 2021, Pathao appointed Chief Financial Officer Fahim Ahmed as MD and CEO. Its current revenue is US$14 million. Patho's vision is to move Bangladesh forward and upward by building the platform for entrepreneurs. A fast-paced organization, the company gives its employees an immense amount of space to grow professionally as well as take ownership of the initiatives undertaken in the organization.

    Research on their competitor

     

    According to DataBD, in Bangladesh, thirty per cent of all online delivery businesses are food delivery. In this country food delivery service started with Hungrynaki, a locally owned food started business in 2013. Six months later, Food-panda launched its operation. Followed by these companies, other food delivery services like Pathao Food, Shohoj Food, etc started their operation. At present, Food panda, Pathao Foods categorical have a presence in the market of Bangladesh. Attributable to the competition in the market, the discount and offers system came. As of now, Food panda is dominating the bulk market share following Shohoz Food within the pipeline. Pathao is within the third food delivery market.

    A report in Dhaka Tribune of May 2019 shows that Pathao Food was the market leader in Bangladesh’s on-demand food delivery services with a market share of 42%. Food Panda had the next biggest chunk at 32%, Hungry Naki at 8%, and Shohoj Food with another 8%.

    The Bangladesh food delivery industry has seen major changes due to Food-panda’s aggressive promotional and marketing campaigns. Their latest campaign was set in motion as Bangladesh Cricket Team Opener Tamim Iqbal played the role of a food delivery man who was taking food delivery orders.

    Hungrynaki also conduct many campaigns like the 'Hungry Naki 11.11 Feast' campaign for food lovers with exciting offers.

    However, Uber Eats were lacking in terms of marketing and advertising in the media. Other companies were also onboarding new partners, and promoting their services through restaurant partnerships.  Uber Eats couldn’t catch up with these types of marketing strategies of the competitors. That's why they have stopped the food delivery service in Bangladesh.

    Pre-production:

    Our team started planning for this OVC back in the beginning of April. First, we decided to fix our agency name along with a tagline for it. Then everyone from our group came up with a list of few names and from that list we decided to set "Gol Morich PR" as our agency name. In our first group meeting, we divided our work equally among us. In primary phase we had three ideas for different brand. One for 7up, second one is for an insurance company and the last one is for Pathao. Among these ideas our team decided to work with Pathao Food delivery service because it completely goes with the Multiculturalism theory. After that I come up with many examples of OVC and documentary related to the OVC which we are planning to make. So, that we can have a clear idea about what kind of work we need to do for our OVC. First, we decided to conduct a campaign on a theme that represent "No work is small". After few brain storming session, we faced some difficulties with the campaign activity. Then we decided to work on a specific point, that is "Mother's Day" OVC campaign. We narrowed down to highlighting the food delivery service through the Mother’s Day campaign. Which will represent the food delivery service along with the multiculturalism concept. At this ideation phase everyone put their same effort in this planning session. 

     

    Once we finalized our ideation, we sat with our course instructor for the final approval on this project. Then Samiul Islam started working on the script and Sadia Simran was responsible for the research and communication & visual sample collection and Ubaid Hassan worked on the casting and location selection. The production equipment rental task was also done by me. For the OVC Sadia Simran also communicated with the Deputy Manager-Human Resources of Pathao Ms. Fariha Ahmed for the approval from the brands side, but they refused to do this for now. So, we planned to do this by ourselves.

    Then we start working on the proposal. Sadia Simran did the research on Pathao and collected information about their brand. Ubaid Hassan did the competitor analysis and I prepared a LOGO and Official Pad for our agency. Ubaid Hassan manage one of his friend to act the main character(boy) of this OVC. Simran decided to work as the mother and Samiul manage one of his friend to work as a cinematographer.

    The most difficult part was to find a proper place for every scene because of our limitation of budget. We were searching for a place like home where we can start shooting according to our script. At the very last moment we all agreed to shoot the indoor part in my house and for the outdoor part we select a road in Mohammudpur Lalmatia.

    Production:

    7 May

    We did the indoor part in that day. We went to my house in the morning and arrange the furniture according to the scene. In the meantime, Samiul Islam and his friend (Sadman) set up all the equipment and double-check it for clarification that everything is working well, Ubaid arrange the props and decorated the room according to our concept. 

    Before we started our shooting Simran did a simple makeup so that she looks like a mother. When we start shooting, we saw that there is not enough light due to the limitation of space and also the lens we are using is not suitable for this scene so, instead of using DSLR camera we used our Mobile phone for the shooting.

    As no one has a professional experience of acting Simran and the Boy character did many mistakes and we need to go through a lot of rehearsal process to get the best possible expression from the actress (simran & her boy).

    We also faced some difficulties in shoot division and angle due to the room size. We only have one decorated room for our shooting and due to this we cannot set our camera according to our script so we had to adjust the scene a little bit according to the situation. As there was a limitation of light, we used mobile flash light when it requires (mostly in close shoot).

    As Simran live far from my house, we finished our shooting in the daylight. After finishing the indoor scenes, we went out to eat something as there were some volunteers with us. We went to a restaurant and discussed about the next part of shooting while enjoying tea after a tiring day. After that everybody went home. 

    17 May

    In this day we decided to do the outdoor shooting part. I and Ubaid with his friend (boy character) went out for shooting. We decided to start our shooting in the morning but due to heavy rain we started in the noon. When we reached the shooting spot, we saw that the place was not that much appropriate as we expected and also the place was crowded, so we had to shift to our 2nd preferred place and there we started our actual shooting. In this part Ubaid himself act as a friend of that boy and i operated the camera.
    The outdoor part was difficult compare to the indoor part because there was always vehicle moving and we can’t have control over the people. Whenever we start shooting people come into frame from here and there. So, we need to take shoots multiple times as we don’t have crew member with us. We also have a regret that we captured a perfect clip but due to the people or other vehicle coming into frame, the clip was unable to use.

    We could not finish our shooting in that day because of the late starting and rain. The day light was almost over and we had 2/3 shoots remaining, so we decided to take that in the next day but unfortunately Ubaid refused to take that part and started giving excuses like, “Complete the post production without that specific part”.  After that it became tough for us to reach him.

     

    25 May

    We needed a small part for the video which will appear on the mobile screen (according to script). Simran tried multiple times to reach out to her cousins who were going to act in that small part but due to some family engagement they were outside of town. But finally, when they came back Simran prepare them for the shooting a take a draft shoot and sent it to Samiul for the confirmation. After the feedback on the draft Simran took the final shoot with her mobile and finalized the clip. 

    She had to shoot this multiple times as they are very young and very noob by their age. Sound quality was not very satisfying but she tried her best to capture the best possible footage and sound from her side.

     

    Post-Production:

    The post production part was done fully by Samiul Islam. After finishing the indoor part of shooting, Samiul edited the video as draft and presented to everyone so that we can understand that we are in the right track or not. Then he worked on editing the outdoor footage and replacing the mobile screen with the footage which Simran took with her cousins. But due to the irresponsive behavior of our team mate Ubaid as he has responsible for the casting management. He and his cast friend were unavailable after the 2nd day of shooting. So, we cannot complete the full production due to lack of footage.

    I also take help from one of my friend to do the sound editing as there were a huge amount of work in the OVC compare to any documentary.

    Production stills:

    We did our pre-production over Google Meet. All our research and ideation part was done by virtual meeting and we finalized our plan in our class at campus B after discussing with our faculty.


    Production:

    We decided to start our shooting before the Eid vacation but due to the rush time, we had to start after the vacation. So we started at 7th of May finish up at 25th May and overall we took 3 days to complete the whole shoot. The shooting experience was very good to all of us. Mostly we faced difficulties in outdoor shooting.
