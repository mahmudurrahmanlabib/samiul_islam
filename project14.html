    Project 14

    Course Code and Name: MSJ11441 Advertising

    Project Name: Animated short film.

    Topic: Problem that arise due to increase in population of Bangladesh.

    Date of Project: Summer 2022

     

    Project Description:

    In this course we were asked to make a video/animation on a given topic ‘Problem that arise due to increase in population of Bangladesh’. We decide to make an animated video which can represent the problem in a very short and effective way so that everyone understand it. To make the animation we shared the storyboard with our facility and get the approval and then our teammate Raian did the research and visual sample collection and after that Sadia did the illustration accordingly. When they are done with their parts I did the animation and sound mixing at the end.

     

    Project Justification:
    Population problem is not a new thing in our country, but we need to stop it if we want to survive. We also need to aware other people about the cause and effects of it. To do so we decided to make an animation cause it’s the only way to compile all the visual element together and present it in a good way, which is not possible in recorded video production. That’s why we did our project by making an animated video.

    Visual Plan

     


    Synopsis:

    This will be a vector based animated video which needs to portray the problems in Bangladesh on due to over population. In this video we will show that due to the increasing amount of population, people are cutting trees, lack of opportunities in the Job market, increase amount of slum dwellers, increasing traffic jam, water pollution and air pollution.

    Camera angle/movement:

    Camera will move from left side to right side just like a Pan shot and, in the frame, we will see the consequences of overpopulation.

    Description of the visual plan:

    In first frame we will show some trees and a man then more man will pop up and the trees will disappear accordingly. 

     

    As the camera will move from left side to right side, an industrial factory will appear in the screen and the people will move infront of the factory to seek job but there will be only limited vacancy as a result

     

     

     


    few will get a better life and rest of them become poor (the visual will show that the poor used to live in a slum).

    In the visual, we will also show that a river which is located just beside the factory, is being polluted by the chemicals and wastes from the factory and the air is polluted by the factory gas.


     

     

    After the that the truck infront of the factory will start to move to the right, as the camera will also moving accordingly. And shows that the increase amount of traffic jam due to over population.

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

     

     


    Then the camera will move upper (tilt up) and in the atmosphere we will show some data and statistic on the screen which will make our audience aware about the consequences of over population.

     

     

     


    Supporting research data:

    As this will be an awareness-based advertisement, we will highlight some recent statistics of Bangladesh to illustrate the alarming situation the nation is in right now. For example-

    1. We will show the current population of Bangladesh which is 168,175,194 and the population density in Bangladesh is 1265 per Km2 (3,277 people per mi2).

    2. The total forest area of Bangladesh is 2.6 million hectares, which is nearly 17.4% of the total land area of the country, whereas the environmentalists consider it essential to have 25%.

    3. The minimum road requirement for any city is 25 percent, whereas the large cities of Bangladesh have only 6 to 7 percent of roads.

    4. The unemployment rate in Bangladesh is increasing rapidly.

    5. Bangladesh is the world’s most polluted country with 76.9 µg/m, while its capital Dhaka became the 2nd worst polluted city, according to the World Air Quality Index (AQI) Report published earlier this week.

    We will also point out the predictions that are made on this country if things keep going this way. For example-

    1. The current population of Bangladesh is 168,179,674 and in 2030 it will be 179.00 million, in 2050 it will increase further to 192.57 million.

    2. It has been estimated that by 2050, one in every seven people in Bangladesh will be displaced by climate change. Specifically, with a projected 19.6 inch (50 cm) rise in sea level, Bangladesh may lose approximately 11% of its land by then, and up to 18 million people may have to migrate because of sea-level rise alone.

    Targeted audience:

    For this advertisement we have several targeted audiences, they are:

    Primary:

    ●       Married couple who tends to take more than 1 child.

    ●       Unemployed poor people who think that more children mean more power & more income in the future.

    ●       Industry owner/ decision maker of any industry. (Industry which pollutes the environment)

    ●       Government people who have the authorities to take initiative.

    Secondary:

    ●       Youth generation - to make the aware so that they can make a better future.

     

    Information will be given:

    Throughout this video we will try show that we have one problem in our country that is “Over population” which is increasing rapidly. But due to this one problem, many problems have raised in our country like deforestation, lack of Job opportunity, increase amount of slum dwellers, traffic jam, water pollution and air pollution etc.

    That statistic we will provide also help our audience to understand the scenario of our country by analyzing the past, present and the future data of our country. 

     

    Persuasion will be occurred:

    The main objective of this advertisement is to make people realize that our future is in danger.  We should take necessary steps to reduce the population of our country. Also, it is one of the major problems of our country. If we overcome this problem, we can get a better and sustainable life.
