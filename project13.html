    Date of Project:

     

    Project Description:

    Our course teacher gave us instructions to write a speech as the course's final assignment while we were still in lockdown during the pandemic. We were instructed to capture the speech in its entirety on camera. However, after being instructed to write down the speech's script, we were required to put on our best apparel, set up a camera in front of us against a suitable backdrop, and begin filming ourselves as though we were speaking to a live audience. Anything may be the subject of the speech as long as it is formal and formatted as a speech.

     

    Project Justification:
    The subject of my speech might sound familiar to everyone, because it’s the truth that people love to hear stories rather than facts and data’s. I am really passionate about storytelling therefore, I decided to speak about a subject that is close to my heart. That’s why I got much time to say and create an audience engaging speech which draw everyone’s attention.

    SPEECH TYPE: PERSUASIVE SPEECH
    SPEECH TITLE: STORYTELLING IS THE BEST WAY TO REDUCE YOUR FEAR & ATRACT AUDIENCE.

     

    My legs are trembling a bit and I am sweating too! And as you can see, I am stuttering a bit as well.

    To be honest, whenever I stand up for public speaking, I become neourvas, like this. It has always made me a little scared. So today I am Samiul Islam come in front of you to talk about how to win this war against this nervousness and ace the stage of public speaking.

    I pretty much sure that I’m not the only one who’s scared of public speaking. Like me many of you are scared of it. But we are living in an independent country and here we have the freedom of speech, right? Then why exactly are we get so scared when it comes to talking in front of an audience? Can any of you give me a reason for that?

    Ok, let me tell you. This nervousness is nothing but something that our mind has conjured up. We are just being self-conscious. We keep doubting ourselves. We keep thinking like, am I saying it right? Will the others listen to me? What will they say? If I mess it up, then people are going to mock me and so on. That’s where our anxiety and nervousness come from. But what happens when we talk to a friend or someone we are comfortable with? Is it the same? No, definitely not. we talk without a hitch. Because we know that person and we have faith in them to not mock me even if you mess up.

    In my opinion the basic difference in these two kinds of speaking is how we deliver. Whenever we think about public speaking the first thing that comes to our mind is, it’s a big deal! We have to maintain proper mannerism and all the other rules that come with it. we have to stick to a specific format, the language has to be neat and we have to deliver it in front of the audience with perfection, and the list just goes on.

    But WHEN ever we are talking to a person who is close to us, we never bother to think even for a heartbeat. We just talk as if we are telling a story. And that storytelling gives us a proper way of communication, we can convey our thoughts just the way we want to. Anxiety, nervousness such concepts just flies out of the window. Tell me one thing, which one is easier to remember? Bullets points from a power point presentation or a simple heart touching story? Of course, it’s the story, right? Because you will forget about all those bullet points unless you have a super strong memory. But when it comes to a story it just engraves itself into peoples’ mind. And above all, it’s far easier to tell a story rather than giving a formal speech.

     

    I guess the obvious question here is, how are we supposed to give a presentation through a story? Ok, let me give me an example. Say, you are presenting a formal guideline for public awareness about Corona Virus, It might not leave an impactful impression. Because presenting a formal guideline means you are addressing the mass in general. What do you think will happen if you convey the same cautionary messages through a story of a small family, like, in a family, what every member Could do to prevent corona virus, what kind of miseries may befall if they get affected, what to do if your child/mother/beloved one get affected with corona virus, what kind of changes should be made in their daily life and so on.  If you can tell a story like that it doesn’t matter if you are talking to hundreds of people at a time, each of them will be able to relate it to their own family life, and while they are busy thinking about their own family they won’t care if you have made any mistake in your presentation. Each of the audience will feel like you are addressing them directly, and that kind of interpersonal communication makes sure that no one is going to lose interest on you, moreover they will start responding to you.

     

    lastly, I would like to emphasize on one thing, public speaking may make us nervous and not everyone maybe able to get rid over it, but we can use this technique to make it better. But we must always keep some basic things in our mind. For example, our stories should exclude any kind racism, sexism, biasness, and inappropriate implications. Most importantly, It must convey our main message to the audience clearly.

    If we can master the art of storytelling, we will be able to present any topic easily and make our audiences understand more effectively. And that will help you to speak perfectly, just like I speak to you.

    Thank you for hearing me. Enjoy the beautiful day.

    SPEECH TITLE: MAINTAIN SOCIAL DISTANCE DUE TO CORONA VIRUS (COVID-19)

     

    Before I start, can i request you for something? Can you please sit at a distance of at least 6 feet from your others? (after sitting at a distance) can anyone guess that why I request you to do this? Well i ask you to do this because of your safety. In a terrible moment, when the whole world is facing a crisis moment due to Corona virus (COVID-19), we all need to practice some certain rules to fight against it. And keep social distance is one of the best practice to prevent Corona Virus. I am Samiul Islam and I am here to discuss about why we should keep social distance and how we can practice it.

    So let’s start from the beginning, what is Corona Virus? According to World Health Organization, “Corona virus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered corona virus.” The corona virus (Covid-19) has started in China and spreading rapidly around the world. A question might be raise in your mind that, “how dangerous is the corona virus?” well i don’t yet know how dangerous the new corona virus is, and i won’t know until more data comes in, but what i know now is, it can make some people a little sick with a slight runny nose. Other people get more sick and can have difficulty to breathing and develop pneumonia. Some people get very sick and need to be hospitalized. Among those, some of them unfortunately died. If we estimates the mortality rate by covid visualizer, in Chain there are 81,736 persons are contaminated and 3,291 persons are dead, in Italy there are 74,386 persons are contaminated and 7,503 persons are dead, in Spain 49,515 persons are contaminated 3,647 persons are dead and in Bangladesh 28 person are active corona patient and 5 person died. Though this number is lower than other countries we should take necessary steps before it’s too late.  So how does it spread? The corona virus spreads mostly through person-to-person contact within about a 6 - feet radius. People with COVID-19, spread viral particles through coughing and sneezing. The particles can land in the mouths or noses of those nearby. It might also be possible to catch by touching a surface where the virus has recently landed and then touching one's mouth, nose or eyes. I have seen that many of the people are using face mask.  Face masks are not effective protection for people who are well, but people who are already ill can wear them to reduce the likelihood that they'll cough or sneeze droplets on loved ones. The best practice is to maintain social distance. You might be thinking that what is social distance? Do we need to stay of our society? Well you don’t need to stay out of the society, the only thing you need to do is creating physical distance from other people, that’s why i ask you to sit at a distance of at least 6 feet.  At the community level, it means closing schools, restaurants, shops, movie theaters, and other places where people gather also not getting together in person with friends, not going to stores unless it is necessary if you can start working from home, not taking public transportation, including bus, Leguna, or rideshares. For those who use to live in hostel, try to avoiding physical contact with your roommates and always use hand sanitizer, mask and try to separate your own necessary things. 

    And if anyone is affected by corona he/she must keep him/her self in self isolation so that he/she may not affects other people. So if you want to live a healthy life and keep your friends, family and beloved person safe please stay home and keep social distance.  Otherwise we all need to suffer together. While staying at home you can watch movies, cook your favorite food, do online conversation, and play online games. You can also share your daily activity on social media so that other people can get motivated to stay home too and enjoy every moment while staying at home.

    Just keep that in mind that if we do not maintain our social distance now, there is a possibility In the future we may not be seen each other again. So stay home, stay safe, keep other safe and have a good day.
