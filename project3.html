Project information

    Project 3

    Course Code and Name: MSJ 11213 Principles of Public Relations

    Project Name: Poster

    Topic: Youth Empowerment

    Date of Project: Fall 2018

     

    Project Description:

    Based on the theme ‘Youth Empowerment’ we choose this topic focused on how the youth generation may use their leadership abilities to bring change in the education system, community relations, and other domains by taking up initiatives and building a more efficient and better nation. We believe Youth Leaders bring the creativity and leadership of the youth population to the forefront. Youth are having training and using their skills for the social welfare and betterment of their country which shows how responsible and powerful our youth are. As a response, we attempted to convey through our poster that- the current generation's youth have taken responsibility for overcoming all boundaries and stereotypes in this so-called society and are working together to establish a great nation. In the poster, there will be an old, broken wall that will represent our Society. The figures on the poster, along with necessary measures such as concrete, brick, and cement block working for re-construction of the wall, will be representing that youth can rebuild the society if they work together. The process of construction of the broken walls will represent the empowerment and the benefit of staying unity together.

     

    Project Justification:
    Since there are still many stereotypes and generalizations exist in our society that need to be overcome, we attempted to show the actual scenario of society using a broken wall. We believed in the idea that they can be changed if the current generation's youth take responsibility for bringing about change as a whole and working together to build a great nation. We've depicted construction parts like concrete, brick, and cement block that were employed by three figures to reconstruct the wall for this purpose. The process of constructing the broken walls will symbolize our youth's empowerment. And the tagline, "Trust in the nation's youth, and they will help us build it one brick at a time," added to the portrayal of youth empowerment.
