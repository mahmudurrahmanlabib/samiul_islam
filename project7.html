    Project 7

    Course Code and Name: MSJ 11202 Bangla for Media

    Project Name: News report Writing

    Project Title:Â 

    Date of Project: Fall 2019

     

    Project Description:

    For use in print media, we were required to generate journalism-related writing. Along with the instructions, a mark scheme was given to us. It was based on the strength of the headline, lead construction, sentence structure, language choice, and of course, proper spelling and punctuation. For this assignment, I read lots of newspapers in both Bangla and English to obtain a better grasp of the news writing process as well as to get a sense of how to frame articles and arrange data for publication on platforms used by the major media. My Bangla vocabulary grew as a result of the writing method analysis, which was really beneficial.

     

    Project Justification:
    To do this task, I had to conduct extensive secondary research on some leading Bangladeshi newspapers. I've learnt how to structure a lead that grab readers attention and got insight into the many writing techniques employed by print media outlets as a result. Because of this project, which forced me to express any story and structured them with relevant information as a result I became better at analyzing Bangla words and sentences.
