    Project Description:

    One of the course's main objectives was to teach us how to compose a professional press release. For which, we were assigned by our then course faculty to write a press release on a designated topic following the format we were given in class. I was asked to prepare on the theme entitled “Being singled out for an accomplishment”. We were expected to prepare a professional press release with proper formatting and style.

     

    Project Justification:
    We did this project during the midst of Covid’19 and at that time educational institutions were closed for over a year already. So, our motive was to arrange something interesting for the school-going youngsters who were getting bored staying at home. For that, nothing except a drawing/ painting competition struck our minds, and to do so we chose the most used color brand during our childhood days, namely, Faber-Castel which made all the teammates nostalgic. Hence, we wanted to do this PR campaign for the Faber-Castel.

    Executive Summary:

    As requested by Faber-Castell Bangladesh, the following public relations proposal has been devised in order to promote and maintain a PR strategy for Faber-Castell Bangladesh successfully.

    Faber-Castell is a renowned stationery brand but, they do not have that much brand recognition among the people of our country. In this difficult situation of the Covid-19 pandemic we have thought to arrange for a painting competition for children online. Children will get to speak their mind through paintings and express their imagination. The event launching will be held through an online platform called Zoom. Parents will encourage their children to take part in this competition. This will be a two months long event. Also, those who will register for the competition will get exciting gift hamper and there are also prize money and scholarship opportunities for children who will stand out among everybody. Throughout the event the progress will be monitored through social media analytics and continuous promotions will be done through social media platforms like Facebook, YouTube, Instagram and also newspapers and FM radio will also be a part of it.

    Situation Analysis:

    Following extensive research and analysis, the circumstances and situation affecting the "    কোথাও আমার হারিয়ে যাওয়ার নেই মানা” campaign appear to be as follows:



    At this time, the COVID-19 pandemic is a limiting factor in the campaign's potential scope. Since the campaign is specifically targeted at youngsters, reaching the target audience on something other than an online platform is currently unlikely.  This pandemic is one of the main factors which is limiting the success of this campaign. The campaign would have been much fruitful if done physically. But, since in this unfavourable situation we all are stressed mentally so, we thought of conducting this campaign to engage children in some fun activity. This situation is very mentally tiring especially for the youngsters. Given that, this world has been suffering for Covid-19 for last one year and recently it's second wave has been more harmful. Young people also seem to be in severe conditions than in the previous year, physicians from every major Covid dedicated hospital have mentioned. Nationally the largest remaining restriction is on education as schools and education establishments remain closed. Considering the fact that schools continue to remain closed, the increasing negative impact this is having on the mental health of children, the exposure to risk that is faced by out-of-school children and the detrimental impact on their cognitive and social development. This campaign would be an initiative to bring some activeness in this current situation.

    SWOT analysis of Faber Castell analyses the brand/company with its strengths, weaknesses, opportunities & threats. SWOT Analysis is a proven management framework which enables a brand like Faber Castell to benchmark its business & performance as compared to the competitors and industry.


    Faber Castell Strengths:

    1. Wide variety of products.
    2. Good quality at affordable price.
    3. Good global distribution network.

    Faber Castell Weaknesses:


    1. Since products are available at different price ranges it is difficult to identify it as premium or non-premium.
    2. Intense competition from other brands as well as local products means limited market share.

    Consumer attitude: There are many copy products of Faber Castell which are not as qualitative as ours so, consumers often mistaken those with ours. But, with our variety of product range customers are quite satisfied and we have 5/5 review on our Facebook official page.

     

    Goals

    In conjunction with Faber-Castell the following goals have been set forth with regards to public relations:

    The main goal of this campaign is to engage the youngsters in something interesting in order to recharge their mood. We got to know how the pandemic affected their mental health like everyone else as well as social distancing and online class made their life tiring and monotonous, stated above. Following these issues to add some dynamic in their regular life we along with Faber-Castell are arranging this particular campaign and we are optimistic about the effectiveness of this campaign as the youngsters will be able to share their untold dreams with others through their paintings. The campaign will also help our client company to bring their quality products in the lime light.

     

     

    Objectives

    I. Engage the youngsters in something interesting in order to recharge their mood

    ● This pandemic created a huge fuss all over the world which led educational institutions to remain closed for over a year now. In the meantime, students are adapting with online education without meeting friends and going for outings. All of them got bored with the social distancing as most of them need to stay home alone when the parents are out for their job. Above all these are ideal for being social media addict or lazy. This campaign will be effective to refresh their mind by adding some fun activities in their current dull life.

    ● The kids will be able to explore new interest, which will help them to keep distance from the electronics device for a while and thus, will be helpful for their health. In addition, this campaign will also bring some joy in the children’s’ life with colors.

    ● Success will be measured through online platforms mostly in Facebook and our website (landing page). We will check the amount of audience/ traffic (youngsters and parents) engagement on our website and social media page.

     

    II. Give wings to the youngster’s dreams

    ● Nowadays children are so caught up with online studies and pandemic. Most of them are panicking due to the current situation that they are no longer able to think of their dream and that is why we are taking such initiatives to remind them about their dreams and ambitions that they need to catch up soon.

    ● Benefit will be same as above and this will also help the kids to work on their dreams during this period which will be helpful for developing themselves.

    ● Success will be measured through online platforms mostly in Facebook and our website (landing page). We will check the amount of audience/ traffic (youngsters and parents) engagement on our website and social media page.

     

    III. Creating awareness of Faber-Castell products

    ● According to our client company’s statement local markets are full of with copy products which are not in good quality and consumers are not aware of that. Many of them are using bad quality products and fear to purchase again which is crashing the consumer base. Moreover, the consumers are also in dilemma due to the variable price of the products. Through this campaign we will aware our target audience about the quality products with reasonable price of Faber-Castle.

    ● Customer will be able to differentiate the copy products from the original ones, which will help our client company to attract a new big demographic. These will be the benefits of our 3rd objective.

    ● Success will be measured through online platforms mostly in Facebook and Faber-Castle’s website. Success will also be measured through the reviews of the products from the participants and others.

     

    Target Audience

    The campaign’s target audience is primarily the urban school going students of Dhaka City. The primary audience would be both male and females from age 14 to 16 from the selected 50 schools of Dhaka city. The socio-economic status would be lower, middle, upper middle and upper class as the students of both public and private schools will be participating in the competition. The main focus would be on the secondary school students and the secondary audience cum the most involved parties- parents as well as the teachers of the children, who can play a vital role to influence the students for participating in the competition. The campaign will create a platform for the children who are into paintings and eager to show their talents as well as for those who got bored from the social isolation even if they are not so good at painting and all of them will get basic drawing kit as a gift hamper in order to appreciate their participation.

     

    Target Media

    The campaign will target social media platforms, more specifically Facebook, due to its wide use within the country as the primary media to reach the audience. We will also be concentrating on reaching the target audience through Instagram and YouTube. Moreover, we will create google adds which will be shown via those social platforms in order to target our audience as well as through our created website the audience will be informed time to time. The campaign also includes sponsored videos from the popular Bangladeshi influencers, who will encourage the youngsters to participate. In addition, advertisements regarding the competition arranged by our clients will be given through newspaper, radio and television. We will also commission a renowned newspaper for a feature writing about the campaign after the competition held and results are out.

     

     

    Strategies and tactics

    Below is the list of stratagies to be used in the promotion of our campaign:

     

    a.      Media outreach

    The targeted media platforms will be as follows:

    Facebook -We will be used the social media platform Facebook to reach our audience. As we are targeting the age group of 14 to 16, they are most likely to use this social media platform in regular basis. So, it will be easier for to connect with them through this platform and reach a grater targeted audience. Also, we can reach our secondary target audience (parents) through this platform easily as well.

    Youtube - The promotional content (mostly videos) will also be posted in our Youtube channel. We will post     influencer videos, user generated contents in this channel which will be also posted in our Facebook page to maximize the reach to our target audience.

    Instagram- Also Ingtagram is one of the popular social media platforms among the youngster right now. We will regularly post about our campaign in our Instagram page. We will also post stories about our activities there. Through this we can be reaching a large group of audience who primarily use Instragram.

    Google Ads- Google ads will also provide an excellent tool to reach our target audience. Through this we can promote our campaign in various online platform and websites.

    Newspaper Ads- Through this we can reach the secondary target audience (parents) who are not necessarily big user of these social media platforms. They can inform our primary audience about the campaign. Thus we can reach more target audience.

    Radio Ads- Fm radio in the Dhaka area is another popular media youngster consume in a regular or semi regular basis. With ads running in this popular fm radio station we can reach more of our target audience.

     

     

    b.      Press Releases

    We will distribute a press release to all the news media houses containing detail of our campaign. Here is the sample press release.

    Faber Castell Bangladesh organized an online campaign launch event for children's painting competition

    May 3rd, 2021, Dhaka, Bangladesh: Faber Castell Bangladesh arranged an online campaign launch event through Zoom which will be held from 3rd May to 10th July called “কোথাও আমার হারিয়ে যাওয়ার নেই মানা”।

    The event was launched to help youngsters to bring out their imaginations through their paintings. This will be a painting competition for them.

    Morshed Mishu, the animator was present in the online event and he said, “Every one of us are facing through really difficult time in this situation especially the children; this competition is a great initiative by Faber Castell Bangladesh to help reducing the boredom a little bit and engage children in some thoughtful activity.”

    This will also encourage the parents to learn about the imaginations of their children.

    This will help reduce the mental stress of the children and they can explore their creative sides.

    c.       Editorials

    We will publish editorials, op-eds in various prestigious newspapers like The Prothom-alo, The Daily Star, The Dhaka Tribune, The Business Standard, Bangladesh protidin etc. The subject matter of these would be to talk about the importance of the imagination of children and how it is important in the mental development of a child. The angel would be especially in the Covid-19 situation how the company is arranging this campaign to help reduce the mantel stress of the children and let them go wherever they want through their imagination. They maybe in home bond by the walls of their room but their mind can go anywhere.

     

    d.      Events

    As June 1st is International Children’s Day, we will arrange a seminar regarding how children can be affected by the pandemic and what should be done to help them free from the mental stress they are having. This would be an excellent opportunity to talk about our campaign and encourage parents to let them participate in this competition. Sheikh Afzal Hossain, Chairman and Professor, Dept. Of fine art, DU, is also going to be there as chief guest to talk about the importance of expressing children’s imagination through art and painting.  Doctors who are expert in the Child psychology will be there to talk about the mental health of the children during this pandemic and healthy ways to express children’s thought. This event will also be broadcasted through Facebook Live.

     

    e.       Speaking Opportunities

    People from the company can tell their stories of their dreams in videos which we will be sharing through our social media. Through this they can also promote how this company is helping children to express themselves. We will also arrange an online website launching event where the people from the company have excellent opportunities to speak about the goals and objectives of their company. How they are working on to be a partner of the children in their journey of imagination.

     

     

     

     

    f.        Social media

    As this campaign is mainly focused on the online activity, we will be using social media platforms like Facebook, Instragram, Youtube to promote and reach our audience through social media posts like posters, videos, user generated contents etc.

     

    g.      Crisis planning

    As children a tent to not like attend competition, they might not be interested at all in our campaign. On top of that most of them are attending class from home with massive workload. In this situation if their parents peruse them to participate, they might not be interested too. If this situation occurred our plan would be to announce that everyone who is going to participate will get a gift box from the company containing color box, pencil, sketch pad etc. As like all of us children like to have gifts they would be interested to participate if the gifts are guaranteed.

     

    Marketing Concept/Key Messages

    Our campaign is focused on the youngsters of age 14 to 16. During this time, they are trapped in their home, way from any activities they are used to. These are making them more mentally stressed out. The campaign is providing an opportunity to let their imagination lose through painting. Let them go wherever their imagination takes them. For these we are mainly using social media platform where most of our interaction is going to be. We are also going to bring many influencers they look up to encourage them in their journey. We are also giving three of them who will be best in expressing their imagination 100000-taka scholarship for one year. We will also give 100 of them who participate in the competition gift box consisting of color box, pencil, sketch pad etc.

    For the key message we chose a line from a song from Rabindranath Tagore which is

            “কোথাও আমার হারিয়ে যাওয়ার নেই মানা”

    Reason for choosing this is that we can be physically bound to one place but our mind can never be bound in one place. We can go wherever we want through our imagination. Especially children can have vivid imagination. This message is telling the children to go wherever they want through their imagination. No one would stop them. This is also why we chose it as our campaign name too.

    Measurements

    Our campaign will be mainly online based. So, during the duration of campaign, we will monitor our social media posts and engagement in these posts. We will also monitor analytics to find out how these posts are doing. During the campaign we will conduct surveys in three phases to see if the campaign is going according to plan. If not we will change the campaign plan based on these data. At the end of the campaign, we will conduct an overall survey to measure the success of our whole campaign. This will help the Clint and also us to conduct future campaign efficiently.
